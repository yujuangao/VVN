Brand

VT color palette, typography rules, and logo usage for all VVN products.

ImportantKey Rules at a Glance
  • Never use colors outside the VVN palette in charts, maps, tables, or layouts
  • Use VT Maroon #861F41 for primary headings, navbars, and accents; VT Orange #E5751F for secondary highlights only
  • Body text is always Charcoal #3D3D3D — never pure black #000000
  • Chart and map titles: headline case · Subtitles and axis labels: sentence case
  • Use the accessible palette when colorblind safety is critical

Color Palette

Brand Colors

VT Maroon #861F41 Primary — navbars, titles, chart fill

VT Orange #E5751F Secondary — highlights, buttons, callouts

Navy #1B5299 Tertiary — chart series, accent

Charcoal #3D3D3D Body text, axis labels

Gray #AAAAAA Captions, secondary text

Light Gray #F7F7F7 Backgrounds, sidebar, table rows

The three colors used in charts and multi-series visualizations are Maroon, Orange, and Navy — access them via scale_fill_vvn("brand").

Palettes

Palette Type Use
brand Categorical 3-group comparisons — maroon, orange, navy
main Categorical General purpose — up to 10 categories
accessible Categorical Colorblind-safe — use when accessibility is critical
maroon_seq Sequential Primary continuous metric — choropleth maps
orange_seq Sequential Secondary continuous metric
navy_seq Sequential Tertiary continuous metric
diverging Diverging Data with a meaningful midpoint (above/below average)
WarningColorblind Safety

VT Maroon and VT Orange can appear identical under red-green colorblindness (~8% of men). When both appear on the same chart, differentiate with shape or line type in addition to color, or use scale_fill_vvn("accessible").

Capitalization Rules

Element Rule Example
Chart title Headline case “Labor Force Participation Rate by Region”
Chart subtitle Sentence case “Virginia counties, 2018–2023”
Axis labels Sentence case “Percentage of households”
Map title Headline case “Median Household Income by County, Virginia”
Map subtitle Sentence case “2020 American Community Survey 5-year estimates”
Table title Headline case “Summary Statistics by County”
Column headings Sentence case “Poverty rate (%)”

Logo Usage

  • Always use the official VT logo alongside VVN branding
  • Do not alter, recolor, or reposition the logo
  • Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the “V” in “Virginia” on all sides
  • The tagline “Invent the Future®” appears beneath the logo on branded materials
